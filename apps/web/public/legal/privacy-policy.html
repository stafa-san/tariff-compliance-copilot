Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

Tariff Copilot ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share your information when you use our mobile application and web platform.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, display name, and authentication credentials via Firebase Authentication.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you use the App, including product descriptions you classify, shipment data you enter, and duty calculations you perform.

Device Information: We may collect device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for app functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App's core functionality (HTS classification, duty calculation, compliance audits)
  • To store your shipment history and compliance reports
  • To process your data through AI services (Anthropic Claude) for classification and audit features
  • To improve and personalize your experience
  • To communicate important updates about the service

3. AI and Third-Party Services

We use Anthropic's Claude AI to provide product classification and compliance audit features. Product descriptions and document content you submit may be processed by this AI service. We do not use your data to train AI models.

We use Firebase (Google Cloud) for authentication, data storage, and hosting.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in Google Firebase (Firestore) with appropriate security rules. We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Firebase/Google Cloud (infrastructure provider)
  • Anthropic (AI processing for classification and audit features)

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date.
